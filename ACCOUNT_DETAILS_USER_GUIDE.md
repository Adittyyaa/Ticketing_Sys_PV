# Account Details - User Guide

## For End Users

### How to Access Your Account Details

1. **Click the Menu Button**
   - Look for the ☰ (hamburger menu) in the top-right corner of the page
   - Click it to open the menu

2. **Select Account Details**
   - You'll see your email and role in the menu
   - Click on **"Account Details"** option

3. **View and Edit Your Profile**
   - Your account information will appear in a modal popup

## Step-by-Step: Editing Your Profile

### Example: Adding Your Phone Number

1. **Open Account Details**
   - Menu (☰) → Account Details

2. **Find Phone Field**
   - Scroll down to the "Phone Number" field
   - It should be empty (or show your current number)

3. **Click and Type**
   - Click in the phone field
   - Type your phone number

4. **Save Changes**
   - Scroll to bottom
   - Click the blue **"Save Changes"** button

5. **Wait for Confirmation**
   - Green success message appears briefly
   - Message says: "Account details updated successfully!"
   - It automatically disappears after 3 seconds

6. **Verify It Saved**
   - Close the modal (click X or Close button)
   - Open Account Details again
   - Your phone number should be there!

## Tips & Tricks

### ✓ Best Practices

- **Keep info current** - Update your phone/name if it changes
- **Professional bio** - Write a 1-2 sentence introduction
- **Use complete phone** - Include country code for international numbers
- **Full company name** - Use official company name, not acronym

### ✗ Common Mistakes

- Forgetting to click **"Save Changes"** (edits won't be saved!)
- Trying to edit email/role (these can't be changed from here)
- Leaving phone field empty (you can if it's optional)
- Very long bio (keep it to a few sentences)

## Troubleshooting

### Q: I edited my name but it didn't save. Why?

**A:** You probably forgot to click **"Save Changes"** button. Your edits are only saved to the database when you click that button.

### Q: Can I change my email?

**A:** No, email is managed by the system admin. Contact your administrator if you need to change it.

### Q: Can I change my role?

**A:** No, only administrators can change user roles. Ask your admin if you need a role change.

### Q: The modal is loading forever. What's wrong?

**A:** 
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page (F5)
- Close and reopen the modal
- If still broken, contact your system administrator

### Q: The green success message showed but I'm not sure if it saved?

**A:** Close the modal and open Account Details again. If your changes are still there, they were saved!

## Getting Help

### If something goes wrong:

1. **Read this guide** - Check if your question is answered above
2. **Close and reopen modal** - Sometimes fixes glitches
3. **Refresh the page** - Reload the entire app
4. **Clear browser cache** - Cmd+Shift+R (Mac) or Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows)
5. **Contact administrator** - If none of above work
